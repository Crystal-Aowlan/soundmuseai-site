About SoundMuseAI

Built by Crystal Aowlan—UK-based artist, music explorer, and self-directed, AI-assisted first-time coder—to provide a human-friendly music mentor for all skill levels, learning styles, and music-making needs.

Whether you're a seasoned musician or just beginning your journey, SoundMuseAI offers a safe, encouraging space for all users—including those who are neurodivergent, disabled, or creatively blocked.

This project began as a vision for something better: a musical co-creator that listens, responds, evolves, and supports—without judgement, jargon, or noise.

SoundMuseAI aims to empower, inspire, and help users find their voice, one sound at a time.
