About SoundMuseAI

SoundMuseAI was created by Crystal Aowlan, a UK-based independent artist, music explorer, and creative polymath. It was designed as a creative companion — a tool that adapts to your needs and skill level, rather than dictating them. Whether you're a seasoned musician or just beginning your journey, SoundMuseAI offers a safe, encouraging space for all users, including those who are neurodivergent, disabled, or creatively blocked.

This project began as a vision for something better: a musical co-creator that listens, responds, evolves, and supports — without judgement, jargon, or noise. SoundMuseAI aims to empower, inspire, and help users find their voice, one sound at a time.
